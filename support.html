Technical Support

Welcome to the Concrete Mix & Cost Calculator support center. Find answers to common questions and learn how to get the most out of the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate concrete volume?

1. Select your shape type (slab, column, wall, stairs, or circle)
2. Enter the dimensions in your preferred unit (metric or imperial)
3. Set the waste percentage (typically 5-10%)
4. Tap "Calculate" to see results including volume, bags needed, and cost

How do I save a project?

After calculating, tap the "Save Project" button in the results screen. Enter a project name (optional) and tap "Save". Your project will be saved to the Projects tab for future reference.

How do I edit a saved project?

Go to the Projects tab, tap on the project you want to edit, then tap the "Edit" button. This will load the project data into the calculator where you can modify dimensions and recalculate.

How do I delete a project?

In the Projects tab, swipe left on the project you want to delete, then tap the "Delete" button. You can also tap "Edit" in the toolbar to select multiple projects for deletion.

How do I use AR measurement?

Tap the AR icon next to any dimension field. Point your camera at the surface, tap to place two points, and the distance will be automatically measured and filled in. Make sure you have good lighting and a clear view of the surface.

How do I export a project to PDF?

Open a saved project, tap the share button, and select "Export PDF". You can then save or share the PDF with project details, calculations, and cost breakdown.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All features work completely offline. No internet connection is required for calculations, project management, or any other functionality.

How accurate are the calculations?

The app uses standard concrete calculation formulas and provides accurate estimates. However, always verify calculations independently and consult with professionals for critical projects. Actual requirements may vary based on site conditions, material properties, and local codes.

Why are my projects not syncing between devices?

All project data is stored locally on your device for privacy and security. Projects are not synced between devices. If you need to transfer projects, you can export them as PDF and recreate them on another device.

What iOS version is required?

Concrete Mix & Cost Calculator requires iOS 15.0 or later. The app is optimized for iPhone and iPad.

How do I change the app language?

The app automatically uses your device's language setting. To change language, go to your iPhone Settings → General → Language & Region, and select your preferred language. The app supports English and Chinese.

Troubleshooting

The app crashes or freezes

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
4. If the problem persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app (note: this will delete all saved projects)

Calculations seem incorrect

Double-check that:
• All dimensions are entered correctly
• The correct unit system is selected (metric/imperial)
• The shape type matches your project
• Waste percentage is appropriate for your project
If you believe there's a calculation error, please report it through the App Store.

AR measurement is not working

Make sure:
• Your device supports ARKit (iPhone 6s or later)
• Camera permissions are enabled in Settings
• You have good lighting conditions
• You're pointing at a clear, visible surface

Tips & Best Practices

Getting accurate estimates

• Measure dimensions carefully and double-check
• Use AR measurement for hard-to-reach areas
• Add 5-10% waste factor for typical projects
• Consider site conditions and access
• Consult with professionals for structural projects

Organizing your projects

• Use descriptive project names (e.g., "Garage Slab - Front", "Patio - Backyard")
• Save projects before making major changes
• Export important projects as PDF for backup
• Review and update cost estimates regularly

Important Disclaimer

This calculator provides estimates for planning purposes only. Actual material requirements may vary based on site conditions, material specifications, and construction methods. Always verify measurements on-site and consult with qualified professionals for structural projects. Order 5-10% extra material to account for waste and variations.
